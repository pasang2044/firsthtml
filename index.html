Here's a time line of Lebron James's Life:

  • 1984 Born in Akron, OH
  • 1998 Lebron James Enter St.Vincent-St.Mary High School
  • 1999 - St.Vincent wins Divison III State Title with Freshman Lebron James.
  • 2001 - St. Vincent Wins Second Division III State Title.
  • 2002 - Sports Illustrated Calls James "The Chosen One"
  • 2003 - Lebron James Signs Endorsement Deals Worth Over 90 Million.
  • 2003 - Lebron James Drafted to the Cleveland Cavaliers.
  • 2004 - Lebron James Wins NBA Rookie of the Year.
  • 2004 - Birth of Lebron James, Jr.
  • 2009 -Lebron James Named 2009 NBA MVP
